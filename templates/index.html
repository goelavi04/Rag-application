⚙️ How it works
1. Upload
Your text is split into chunks and each chunk is converted to a vector using sentence-transformers running locally.
2. Search
Your question is also converted to a vector. Pinecone finds the 3 most similar chunks using cosine similarity.
3. Answer
The relevant chunks + your question go to Llama 3.3 via OpenRouter. The AI answers using only your content.
